What i reccomend:
Minimum of 4GB RAM for web-browsing, 8GB if you use it for light gaming, 16GB+ compiling or editing projects while stuff runs simultaneously
Intel Core for most tasks (gaming etc.), for web-browsing i would say get a Pentium and buy a GPU too.
If you don't care about portability, get a cheap refurbished PC (Thinkcentres are probably your best choice, mine costed 30$), but if you want a laptop, try looking at Thinkpads or Toshiba laptops. You won't get massive performance out of them (30$ Pentium pc > 100$ Atom laptop).
If you do plan on getting a PC, open it up first. Because you probably plan on getting a cheap refurb, on some of them the BIOS has a password. No problem though, just open it up, remove the battery, let it sit for 10 seconds and boom! No more password.
Next step, install your favourite OS on it. Use a guide.
And thats kinda it. You have a working PC (or maybe not). If your PC works, but no video out, buy a GPU and install it.
